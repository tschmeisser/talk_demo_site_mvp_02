Our Story

From a backyard grill to Portland's favorite burger spot.

It Started with a Smash

In 2019, chef Marcus Rivera started smashing burgers at the Portland Saturday Market out of a tiny pop-up tent. Armed with a cast iron griddle, a cooler full of fresh-ground beef, and a dream, he served 50 burgers that first weekend. They sold out in two hours.

Word spread fast. The line got longer every week. Food bloggers showed up. Then the Oregonian. By 2020, Marcus and his partner Elena had enough of a following to open a brick-and-mortar spot on SE Hawthorne.

Today, Smash Stack Burger Co. serves hundreds of burgers daily, but the mission hasn't changed: fresh ingredients, simple technique, and flavors that make people come back.

The Smash Stack Way

We keep things simple because that's when burgers are at their best. Our beef comes from Painted Hills Natural Beef in Fossil, Oregon - pasture-raised, no antibiotics, no hormones.

We hand-form every ball of beef and smash it flat on a 450-degree griddle. That's where the magic happens. The Maillard reaction creates a crust of caramelized, crispy beef flavor that you can't get any other way.

Our buns come from a local bakery. Our pickles are brined in-house. Our sauces are made from scratch every morning. It takes more work, but you can taste the difference.
